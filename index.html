Tiger

Tigers are the largest wild cats in the world and can roar loud enough to be heard miles away.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Average Length: 8 - 10 feet
  • Lifespan: 10 - 15 years
  • Habitat: Forests and grasslands

Tigers are powerful predators known for their orange fur with black stripes. They are excellent swimmers and usually hunt alone in the wild.
